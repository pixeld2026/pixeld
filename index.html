Innovation - SleepArt

The WRO topic that inspired us the most was "People, Robots and AI". After many ideas, we created our final project: SleepArt.

SleepArt is an interactive visual robot that monitors biological conditions during sleep using biosensor fusion technology.

The collected data is transformed into a unique visual artwork using mathematical models and algorithms.

Instead of only showing statistics and graphs, SleepArt turns human biological processes into art.

Our goal is to raise awareness about sleep and mental health. We want to connect technology, creativity and human wellbeing.

SleepArt shows that everyone can create something unique, without needing artistic experience.
