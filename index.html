Innovation - SleepArt

Among the WRO topics, we connected most with "People, Robots and AI". After a long search, we created our final idea: SleepArt.

SleepArt is an interactive visual robot that uses biosensor fusion technology to monitor biological conditions during sleep. The collected data is transformed into a unique visual artwork using mathematical models and algorithms.

Instead of showing only statistics and graphs, SleepArt turns human biological processes into art.

Our goal is to raise awareness about sleep and mental health. Sleep problems and stress affect millions of people around the world.

We wanted to create more than just a health device. We wanted to create an emotional experience that connects technology, art, and human wellbeing.

SleepArt also represents equality: anyone can create their own artwork without needing artistic skills.

For us, SleepArt is not only a robotics project. It is an experiment to combine technology, creativity, and human health into something meaningful.
